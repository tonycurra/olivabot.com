Autonomous Olive Harvesting for
Traditional Groves.

Olivabot is an autonomous arboreal climbing robot designed for traditional and steep-slope olive groves.

Reserve Slot How it works

We build for the land where tractors cannot go. Olivabot identifies ripe fruit using AI, climbs the tree without damaging the bark, and harvests autonomously—eliminating the need for manual labor in challenging terrains.

Olivabot GB1 field tests — Southern Italy, Aug 2025

How it Works

Four simple phases take the GB4 from sensing to harvest and back to charge—farmers and investors can follow the logic at a glance.

📡

Digital Scout

360° scan maps trunk, harvest zones and ripeness while flagging pests or disease.

🐒

Gibbon‑Grip

Bio‑inspired limbs climb the tree; no soil compaction and AI reroutes around weak branches.

🛠️

Precision Harvest

Tools shake or comb fruit into your ground nets with 95%+ accuracy—no bark damage.

☀️

Solar Cycle

At 15% battery the unit returns to its solar “House” for a sodium‑ion recharge; IR mode enables night work.
