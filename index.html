Olivabot

Bio-inspired robots for olive harvesting

About

At Olivabot, we are developing an robotic solution for olive harvesting in traditional olive groves. Our robots are inspired by the natural movements of animals, allowing them to navigate complex terrains and efficiently harvest olives without damaging the trees.

Olivabot will be commercially available approximatively in 2028. For info and early testing get in touch: hello@olivabot.com

Services

We provide consultancy services, prototypes and MVPs for early stage startups.

  • Robotics design and development
  • Sensor integration and data analysis
  • Field testing and optimization
