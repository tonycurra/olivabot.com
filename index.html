No workers?
No problem.

Olivabot offers automated harvesting services for traditional olive groves with steep-slope terrains and century-old trees. Harvest like your grandpa, with modern tools.

How it works

We build for the land where tractors cannot go. Olivabot identifies ripe fruit using AI, climbs the tree without damaging the bark, and harvests autonomously—eliminating the need for manual labor in challenging terrains.

Olivabot gb1, field tests — Southern Italy, Aug 2025


Olivabot GB1 (tech name: Gibbon Bot mk.1) is our first gibbon-inspired robot, designed for century old trees and sloped terrains. Photos taken during a field test in southern Italy, summer 2025. Gibbon Bot mk.1 shows promising stability and mobility on olive trees.
